Privacy and Terms

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) sets out the basis on which we collect, process, use, share and protect Personal Data in connection with the use of our application (“App”) by you (“you”, or “your store”).

Please read this Policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your Personal Data and how we will treat it. This Policy applies to our App only. Consent

Information we collect

The personal information that you are asked to provide, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be made clear to you at the point we ask you to provide your personal information.

If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

Permissions

When you install the App, we are automatically able to access certain types of information from your e-commerce store. The app does not share your information without your specific action or explicit request with the advertising platforms. Below given is the list of Permissions we take about your store and its information and products.

Store Information

  • We are collecting basic information from a store like Shop Owner’s Name, Store Name, Store Domain, Address, Contact Email, Support Email, Phone No, Store Plan, etc. This information shall be used to:

  • Create an account on our App.

  • Send a notification email to the store owner whenever there is a process completion or some error in the product data feed. 

  • We use this personally identifiable information of store owners to have effective communication related to different processes on our app. 

  • We never send any promotional emails related to cross-selling of our services or launching any other service that is not in the scope of the app. 

  • We do send emails related to updates launched, bug fixes, updates in the documentation, and any other change that may affect the utility of this app.

Read Products

These permissions allow us to read the product information like product Title, Description, Images, Prices, Barcode and SKUs, Variants, and their details, etc... However, this permission does not get us access to any Personally Identifiable Information of any individual.

Read/Write Theme

This permission is taken to re-verify the store in the event of a theme change. Whenever any store changes the theme and has used the HTML tag method of verification, the App re-inserts the HTML tag to keep the store verified and claimed in the Merchant Center. That avoids the downtime of the Advertising Campaigns.

About the information related to any identifiable individual

In most of the use cases, we don't collect, process, or store any information that can be considered personally identifiable information of any customer of our app user. However, we take access to the order and customer data of a merchant when the merchant decides to use Additional features like, 

Google Calculated Delivery Speed Estimates: we have to collect the order details like the date of the order, shipping address zip-codes, and fulfill details to share with Google for the "Google Calculated Delivery Speed Estimates” feature. We take permission for access to such details, and by default, we get access to all the details of the order including the customer's information. However, we don't process such personally identifiable information of the customers of a merchant nor do we store it on any of our servers or repository.

Simprosys' Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt out of certain options.

To determine the appropriate retention period for Personal Data, we consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the Personal Data, the potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure of your Personal Data, the purposes for which we process your Personal Data, and whether we can achieve those purposes through other means, and the applicable legal requirements.

When you choose to connect a 3rd party account / any advertising platforms (example: Google, Microsoft and Facebook), we store the tokens provided to the app by the 3rd party to be used for the services provided by the app, e.g. create product feeds and/or ad campaigns.

When you choose to uninstall the app, we keep your personal information saved for 30 days. Past these 30 days, all your data will be automatically deleted from the database.

Data Processed via Google's API

Our App places a high value on your privacy and treats your data with the utmost care and responsibility. Any data we obtain through Google APIs is used by our app or shared with other applications in strict adherence to the Google API Services User Data Policy. This includes strict compliance with the policy's Limited Use requirements. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.

Simprosys Google Shopping Feed does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

The services offered by our app are based on a subscription basis ("Subscription(s)"). You will be billed in advance on a recurring and periodic basis ("Billing Cycle"). Billing cycles are set on a monthly basis.

At the end of each Billing Cycle, your Subscription will automatically renew under the exact same conditions unless you cancel/upgrade it or Simprosys Google Shopping Feed  cancels it on your request. You may cancel your Subscription renewal through your Shopline store itself.

The app offers you a 21-days free trial to get familiar with the features and offerings. A trial extension will be provided by our team on a case-to-case basis after reviewing the account.

The free trial begins as soon as the app is installed on your Shopline store. Simprosys Google Shopping Feed is not bound to provide a trial extension in any case.

Merchants already on paid subscriptions shall remain on the same old plan even if we implement a new pricing structure as long as the app remains installed on their store. Once the app is deactivated/re-activated or uninstalled/re-installed or plan is upgraded/downgraded they will have to approve the subscription charges as per the new pricing structure.

Our team will provide you with reasonable prior notice of any change in Subscription fees to give you an opportunity to terminate your Subscription before such change becomes effective.
