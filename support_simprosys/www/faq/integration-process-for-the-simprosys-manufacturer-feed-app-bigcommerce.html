Step-by-Step Integration Guide for the Simprosys Manufacturer Feed App for BigCommerce

Apr 29, 2025

The Simprosys Manufacturer Feed app helps manufacturers provide rich, detailed, and accurate product information to Google Manufacturer Center.

Since our app works on the Content API method, it will automatically sync product data from your BigCommerce store and update it to your Google Manufacturer Center without any manual intervention.

This guide will help you understand the process of integrating the Simprosys Manufacturer Feed app into your BigCommerce Store.

Installation Process

  1. Open your BigCommerce store and navigate to the Apps section.
  2. Click on Apps Marketplace.
  3. Search for Simprosys Manufacturer Feed in the BigCommerce App Store.
  4. Click on Get This App. This will redirect you to the BigCommerce installation page.
  5. Click on Install and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Once installed, the app interface will open, where the first step is to log in to your Google Account.

Connecting Your Google Manufacturer Center

  1. Click on Sign in with Google and select the required Google account with admin access to your Google Manufacturer Center.
  2. Grant the required permissions by clicking on Allow.
  3. You will be redirected to the app interface.
  4. Enter the Manufacturer Center Account ID for which you would like to submit the feed and click on the Confirm button.

Setting Up Your Preferences

  1. Select preferences for:
  2. Products
  3. Brands
  4. Product Titles & Descriptions
  5. Image Preferences
  6. Important: Choose the Product ID format carefully, as it cannot be changed once approved in the Google Manufacturer Center.
  7. Click Sync to proceed.

Syncing Products

  • The products from your BigCommerce store will now be synced into the app.
  • The syncing process may take some time, depending on the number of published products in your store.

Initially, the status will be shown as Pending, which will change to Submitted once they are successfully synced and submitted to your Manufacturer Center.

For further information regarding the Simprosys Manufacturer Feed app, you may reach out to us at support@simprosys.com.
