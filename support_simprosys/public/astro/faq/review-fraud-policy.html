DEFINITIONS

For the purposes of this Policy, the following terms shall have the meanings ascribed to them below unless the context otherwise requires:

  • “Review” means any written, visual, or audio content, rating, feedback, testimonial, or other form of evaluation posted by Users on the Platform relating to products, services, merchants, or the Platform itself.
  • “Review Fraud” means any act or omission that involves the submission, solicitation, or manipulation of reviews that are false, misleading, fabricated, incentivized without disclosure, or otherwise designed to misrepresent the genuine experience or quality of the subject matter of the review.
  • “User” means any individual, entity, merchant, third-party service provider, or other party who accesses or uses the Platform or submits reviews thereto.

PURPOSE AND APPLICATION

This Policy is intended to maintain and promote a fair, transparent, and reliable review environment on the Platform, safeguarding the interests of all Users and ensuring compliance with applicable laws and industry standards. This Policy applies to all Users who post, solicit, manage, or interact with Reviews on or through the Platform, regardless of the mode or origin of such Reviews.

PROHIBITED REVIEW CONDUCT

Users shall strictly refrain from engaging in any acts constituting Review Fraud, including but not limited to:

  • Submitting or facilitating the submission of Reviews that are knowingly false, misleading, fabricated, or lacking any bona fide experience with the product or service reviewed.
  • Offering, providing, or accepting any form of compensation, incentive, or benefit in exchange for Reviews, except where such incentives are explicitly permitted, fully disclosed, and compliant with applicable legal requirements.
  • Utilizing multiple identities, accounts, or personas to post duplicate, conflicting, or misleading Reviews for the same subject matter.
  • Altering, removing, suppressing, or otherwise manipulating Reviews to distort genuine consumer opinion or product representation.
  • Posting content within Reviews that is unlawful, defamatory, obscene, harassing, discriminatory, promotional, or in violation of any applicable laws or Platform standards.
  • Submitting Reviews by parties having a conflict of interest, including but not limited to employees, affiliates, family members, or competitors of the merchant or product subject to the Review.

REVIEW COLLECTION, VERIFICATION, AND MONITORING

The Company endeavors to ensure that all Reviews collected through the Platform are genuine and represent the honest opinions of verified purchasers or users. Reviews solicited via automated, permission-based communications sent to verified customers shall be considered verified Reviews. Reviews imported from external sources shall be accepted without modification but are subject to the same standards and scrutiny as original Reviews.

The Company reserves the absolute right to monitor, verify, and investigate the authenticity of all Reviews through automated means, manual inspections, or other methods it deems appropriate. Reviews found to be non-compliant with this Policy may be removed, flagged, or withheld from display without prior notice.

ENFORCEMENT AND REMEDIAL MEASURES

To protect the integrity of the Platform and its Users, the Company reserves the right to take any and all necessary actions in response to violations of this Policy, including but not limited to:

  • Removing, disabling, or otherwise restricting access to fraudulent or non-compliant Reviews.
  • Suspending, restricting, or terminating the accounts of Users or merchants engaged in Review Fraud or related misconduct.
  • Reporting illegal or egregious activities to appropriate regulatory, law enforcement, or governmental authorities.
  • Exercising all other rights and remedies available under applicable law, including claims for damages, injunctions, or other equitable relief.

USER REPORTING OBLIGATIONS

Users are encouraged to report any suspected instances of Review Fraud or violations of this Policy promptly by contacting the Company at support@proviews.com. The Company commits to treating all reports with confidentiality and investigating them thoroughly.
